Pre-SignUp with Dombox

"Pre-SignUp with Dombox" is an entrepreneur-friendly variation of our product "Subscribe with Dombox".

Budding entrepreneurs usually don't have enough money until they bring investors on-board. While building their product, they can now accept pre-signups without spending a dime.

Demo

Add Button

The following two lines of code will display our "Pre-Signup with Dombox" button in your website or app. Note: if you remove the "data-presignup" attribute, the button will be displayed as "Subscribe with Dombox". 

                    
<div class="subscribe-with-dombox" data-presignup="true"></div>
<script type="text/javascript" src="//domboxmail.com/sublet.js"></script>
